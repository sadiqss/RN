THE BLOG OF SADIQUR RAHMAN

Sadiqur Rahman is a Renewable Energy Engineer. Modelling and Simulation is the main focus of his academic endeavour. Time to time he worked in different laboratory. Some his works are on Vertical farming, Cold Storage and recently on industrial oven. He had knack on different simulation software such as Catia, Star CCM, Ansys and Solid Works. Other than this he is passionate for web developing. He loves to read and travel.
